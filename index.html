Welcome to Rip Boosters!

Remember the thrill of walking out of the local hobby shop, tearing open a fresh foil pack, and hunting for that elusive insert card?

Rip Boosters is a digital passion project built to recreate that exact pack-opening magic. Whether you are here to trip down memory lane with classic late '80s and '90s nostalgia or you want to rip into high-gloss modern sets, we’ve got you covered. Dive into full collections complete with accurate pack sizes, authentic pull rates, and high-quality card backs.

Pick your favorite era, cross your fingers for a big hit, and happy collecting!
