About Rip Boosters

Hey there, welcome to Rip Boosters! This site is a completely free, non-commercial passion project built for anyone who loves trading cards and the classic rush of opening a fresh pack. Whether you're chasing the Power Nine in Magic: The Gathering sets, hunting for Spectra Hits in Marvel Masterpieces, or looking for retro Garbage Pail Kids stickers and Masters of the Universe cards, we've built this simulator to recreate those exact card-opening experiences online.

Authentic Pull Simulation We structure our packs around accurate set checklists, historic rarity drop distributions, and true pull ratios.
Interactive Card Art Click or tap any card you pull to launch a deep lightbox view, letting you check out the high-quality front and back artwork details.
Local Progress Tracking Your collection binder counts are tracked dynamically and stored right in your local browser storage cache so you don't lose your progress.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Rip Boosters. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Rip Boosters and how it is used.

1. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

This website uses Google AdSense, a web advertising service provided by Google, Inc. Google uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or by utilizing standard browser tracking opt-out preferences.

2. Web Analytics Tracking

We leverage automated analytics tools including Cloudflare Web Analytics/Zaraz and Microsoft Clarity to analyze user movement and optimize site layout experiences. These services collect network metrics, timing indicators, mouse movements, scrolling interactions, and baseline click patterns. This data is entirely anonymized and is used solely to spot client-side application performance issues or evaluate trading card pack simulation popularity trends.

3. Local Storage Architecture

Rip Boosters saves your simulation collection milestone data (such as card pull records, counts, and completed binder checklists) directly within your browser's local storage database (LocalStorage). This data remains isolated entirely to your local device hardware and is never synchronized to backend system servers managed by us.

4. External Links

Our site contains links to external platforms including Google Forms, Ko-fi, and the Scryfall API. Once you leave our domain, we do not maintain authority over third-party data processing procedures.
